Achievments

Skyview achieved a recycling rate of 65 percent.

In March 2013, Skyview received the U.S. Green Ribbon School award for reducing environmental impacts, including waste reduction, energy and water conservation, for excellence in health and wellness programs for students and staff, and for excellent environmental and sustainability education.






What's New

Skyview National Junior Honor Society

Congratulations to all National Junior Honor Society members this year!

Students recognized achieved a minimum of a 3.5 cumulative GPA and 15 hours of community of service. There will be a pre-recorded virtual ceremony induction ceremony posted on the Skyview NJHS website on Wednesday, May 26th. Inductees received an email last Friday with details regarding the ceremony and certificates. This year we have 138 members and over 2400 hours served. See attached for the complete list of this year's NJHS members. Great job, Eagles!





Skyview 8th Graders Headed to National History Day Competition

Skyview's Bryan and Brandon Nie (8th grade) earned 2nd place at the National History Day WA State competition earlier this month, and qualified for Nationals! Normally this would mean a week in D.C. in June- this year it is all virtual.
Additionally, they were awarded the Lyle L. Nelson Research Award which recognizes an outstanding entry in naval, maritime, or military history and comes with a cash prize.
